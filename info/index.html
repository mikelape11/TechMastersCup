Tech Masters Cup is finally here!

Informations:

  • Each team is form of 2 players!

  • All the teams will compete in Brand new Tech Maps ( 42s - 52s ) !

Phase 1 - The Qualifiers (4th - 25th Sep)

  • As a registration all interested teams have to state in #registration their Team Name and their 2 members names.

  • For three straight weeks 2-3 new Maps will be added to the Tech Masters Cup Server!

  • The goal for each team is to manage to have as much as possible highest rank from the whole Map Pack (The average from 2 players) !

  • Teams that do not finished all maps by the end of Phase 1, may be cancel!

  • By the end of Phase 1 we pick the Top Teams (depending on participants) and we Qualified them to Phase 2!

Phase 2 - The Live Matches

  • We gather the Top Teams and we creating and publishing a schedule by the lottery. (Times and dates of the event could be flexible for the players except in some situations).

  • With Teams Mode of 2vs2, players are calling to face all the Map Pack live for Specific Rounds (exp first team to 7). *Teams can have substitutes on Phase 2 , but with players that are not involved in any other teams!

  • Winning Teams will face other Winning Teams until the end.

  • Semi Finals and Finals will have on addition more details for the matches, but it will be announce when the time comes!

The registrations will start from 1st and until 3rd of September! (Note : By this period the first maps may have already be uploaded)

More may be added, but the prize for now is:

  • 100.000 coopers (By Team Xtreme)
